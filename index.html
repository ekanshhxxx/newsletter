The Daily Bugle is a daily newspaper that has been in publication since 1898. Founded by J. Jonah Jameson, the paper has a long history of reporting on the latest news and events in New York City. With a tabloid format, the Daily Bugle is known for its sensational headlines and in-depth coverage of the city's superhero community, often taking a critical stance towards figures like Spider-Man. The paper is headquartered in the iconic Daily Bugle Building, a 46-story complex in Manhattan. Over the years, the Daily Bugle has expanded to include various publications and media outlets, solidifying its position as a leading source of news and information in the city.
